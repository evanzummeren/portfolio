About

I'm a Dutch journalist, designer-developer. Lately I have mostly been focussing on digital storytelling. I know this term sounds kinda lame. I studied History (BA) and Journalism (MA) at the University of Amsterdam.

I currently work as a freelancer. Lately I have been working for Campscapes/iC-ACCESS, the Expertise Centre for Journalism, Pieter van Huystee, Stichting Verhalende Journalistiek, Institute for Human Activities and Nieuwsuur.

I was a student-assistent at Coding the Humanities (UvA) and CREATE (UvA). I have been interning at VPRO Digitaal/Tegenlicht as a creative coder and as a researcher at Pieter van Huystee.

A full resume is available upon request.
